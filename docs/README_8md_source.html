10 ReChain is a modified blockchain design that uses signature records to enforce blockchain
11 integrity rather than an expensive proof-of-work calculation (although there is still a fixed
12 proof-of-work to stop clients from creating large numbers of blocks at once). The goal is to
13 create a network of clients that share the burden of hosting, publishing and peer-reviewing
14 a repository of research documents in an immutable blockchain.
